The Problem

Modern phones bombard you with notifications. Modern assistants do the opposite — they stay silent until you explicitly wake them. Both extremes are broken. You're either drowning in noise or getting no help when you actually need it.

The Solution

AURA computes the cost of staying silent against the burden of speaking on every potential nudge. It speaks only when the math says silence would be worse. Every decision is logged and inspectable.
